What’s the first thing you do when you are looking to avail a service or Buy a Product? Search it on Google, right? SEO does exactly this for you but in opposite direction. Through SEO i.e. Search Engine Optimization, you as a seller reach the interested customers, when they are searching for any service or product on Google, Yahoo and other Search Engines.

But, question is what after that? User may have visited your website, liked your products and have planned to make a purchase later. What will bring these interested customers back to your website? It’s SMO i.e. Social Media Optimization. People spend a considerable time on Social Media these days and it’s best to catch them right there after they have expressed interest in your offerings on the website. Thus SEO and SMO are backbone of Digital Marketing.
